How do I create a playlist?

To create one:
1. Tap Your Library.
2. Tap CREATE.
3. Give your playlist a name.
4. Start adding songs
(and we'll help you along).

Is it only possible to shuffle play music?

Any playlist with the shuffle icon
will play on shuffle. Some playlists won't have the shuffle icon, so you can tap any song to play it.

How do I activate Data Saver mode?

1. Tap Home.
2. Tap Settings.
3. Tap Data Saver.
4. Switch on Data Saver.
